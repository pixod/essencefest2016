The high science of Ma'at and Kenny


The heart is weighed against the feather of truth by the goddess Ma'at, in the presence of Thoth and Sebek. From the Papyrus of Ani.

There are several mathematical descriptions that result in approximately heart-shaped curves. The best-known of these is the cardioid, which is an epicycloid with one cusp. The implicit curve (x2+y2-1)3-x2y3=0 yields a better approximation of the heart shape.[7]
